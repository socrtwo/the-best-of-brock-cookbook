California Mango Chicken Salad

Scale this recipe Timer Shopping List Convert
6 lbs (1 lb) chicken, cooked and diced

½ cup mango, sm dice

½ cup (4 tsp) cilantro, chopped

½ cup (4 tsp) jalapeños, chopped

½ tsp salt and pepper

1 tsp chili powder

¼ tsp cumin

½ tsp coriander

2 sweet (3 tbsp) red peppers, finely diced

¼ cup (2 tsp) honey

½ cup (4 tsp) Dijon mustard

¾ cup (2 tbsp) mayonnaise


Mix all ingredients well.

Serve with your favorite bread or sandwich roll.


Yield: 5 servings (6–7 sandwiches)

Eric Lindholm
