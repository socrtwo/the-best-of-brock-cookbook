Carolina Cabbage

¼ lg heads green cabbage

½ red bell pepper

½ green bell pepper

¼ onions, lg

¼ head broccoli

0 pint vegetable oil

⅛ lb turkey bacon

Mrs. Dash seasoning to taste

Salt to taste

Pepper to taste


Cut the cabbage in small dice.

Remove the seeds from the peppers and dice.

Dice the onion.

Remove the broccoli florets from the stalk.

Combine all ingredients and adjust seasoning.

Allow to set in the refrigerator until flavors have combined.

Serve.


Yield: 5 servings
