Pepperoni Dip

Scale this recipe Timer Shopping List Convert

1 (4 oz) package turkey pepperoni, chopped

1 (8 oz) package light cream cheese

½ can condensed cream of mushroom soup


Place soup and cream cheese into microwaveable bowl. 

Cover and heat on high for 1 minute, stir and heat until melted together, approximately another 1–2 minutes.

Add chopped pepperoni. 

Stir. 

Cover and microwave for another minute.

Serve with crackers, pita chips, or mini bagels.


Yield: 5 servings

Chuck Staab
