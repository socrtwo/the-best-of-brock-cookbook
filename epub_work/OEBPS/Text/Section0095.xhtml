Peach Salsa

Scale this recipe Timer Shopping List Convert

1 ½ cups sliced peaches, diced

⅓ cup red bell pepper, diced

1 bunch (¼ cup) green onions, sliced

2 ½ tsp jalapeño pepper, diced, no seeds

⅔ cup vine-ripened tomatoes, deseeded and diced

Juice of two limes

1 tbsp cilantro

Salt, to taste


Add all ingredients and let stand overnight.

Serve with crab cakes.


Yield: 5 servings

Michael Hamilton
