Sweet Milk Griddle Cakes

Scale this recipe Timer Shopping List Convert

2 cups flour

1 tsp salt

1 ½ tsp baking powder

2 tbsp sugar

2 cups milk

1 egg

1 tbsp butter


Mix and sift flour, salt, baking powder and sugar. 

Add milk, egg, and butter. 

Mix well.

Drop by spoonfuls onto a lightly greased hot griddle. 

When puffed full of bubbles and cooked on edges, turn and cook on the other side.


Portions: 10 med cakes

Renee Bloch
