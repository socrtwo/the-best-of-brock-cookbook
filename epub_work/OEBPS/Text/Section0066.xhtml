Cream Cheese Flan

Scale this recipe Timer Shopping List Convert

⅓ tsp sugar

1 tbsp water

1 ¼ eggs

5 ⅝ oz cream cheese

½ tsp vanilla

⅓ can evaporated milk

⅓ can condensed milk


Preheat oven to 400° F.

On stovetop, heat the sugar and water to caramelize. 

Pour caramelized mixture into an 8" x 8" baking dish or mold covering the bottom and sides. 

Once the mold is covered, remove from heat and allow to cool and harden.

Place the remaining ingredients in a mixer and incorporate thoroughly. 

Pour the mixture into the caramelized lined mold.

Place the mold in a larger pan that can be filled half way up the side of the flan mold with water. 

Place in a preheated oven at 400° F.

Bake for 30–35 minutes at 400° F. 

Check doneness with a toothpick, which should come out clean.

Remove flan from the oven. 

Allow to cool completely and then refrigerate for 2 hours.

Cut 2" x 2" squares giving 16 portions.


Yield: 5 servings
