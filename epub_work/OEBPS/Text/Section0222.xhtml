½ bunch Italian parsley

½ bunch cilantro

½ bunch spinach

1 sm bunch dill

½ cup white rice

1 egg

1 quart plain yogurt

2 cups water

3 tbsp flour


Cut up greens and set aside.

In a small bowl, mix rice and egg. 

In a large bowl mix yogurt, water and flour. 

Add rice to yogurt mixture and put on stove. 

Bring to a boil, stirring constantly.

When rice is soft, add greens and keep stirring. 

When greens are soft serve in small bowls or glasses, with or without spoons.


Yield: 4 servings
