Pizza Dough

1 pint warm water

2 tsp dry yeast

1 tsp sugar

1 oz olive oil

1 tbsp kosher salt

2 lbs high gluten flour


Combine the water, yeast and sugar; let sit until it becomes a foamy mix (2 minutes or more usually).

Add oil and salt; mix well.

Gradually add flour to mix. 

Mix with dough hook for 8 minutes until dough pulls away from the mixing bowl.

[It may be necessary to add more flour during mixing.]

Cut dough into 20 oz balls. Rub with olive oil and refrigerate until ready to use.

Can be made with the Pizza Sauce in the Dips and Sauces section!


Yield: 2, 20 oz dough balls

(2 pizzas of 8 slices)

Tony Paterno
