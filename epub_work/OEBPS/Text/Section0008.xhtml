Homemade Spaghetti and Meatballs

2 (10 ¾ oz) cans condensed tomato soup, undiluted or 2 cans tomato, chopped

2 ¾ cups water

1 (12 oz) can tomato paste

1 (4 ½ oz) jar sliced mushrooms, undrained

1 med onion, chopped

3 tbsp Worcestershire sauce

3 tbsp chili powder

1 tsp salt

½ tsp cayenne pepper

2 garlic cloves, minced

Pinch pepper

2 lbs ground beef

2 eggs, beaten

¼ cup onion, chopped

1 tsp garlic salt

½ tsp pepper


In a large Dutch oven or kettle, combine first 12 ingredients.

Simmer uncovered for 2 hours stirring occasionally.

In a bowl combine the beef, eggs, onion, garlic salt and pepper.

Shape into meatballs.

Brown in skillet.

Add meatballs to sauce and simmer for 1 hour.

Served over spaghetti.

Can also be served with fresh salad and garlic breadsticks.


Yield: 6 servings
