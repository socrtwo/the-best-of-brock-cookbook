Texas Style BBQ Brisket

Kitchen Timer     Recipe Multiplier     Shopping List     Unit Converter

                                                                    


5 lbs beef brisket, layer ¼" thick of fat

1 tbsp each of salt, sugar and ground pepper

2 tbsp chili powder

1 tsp cumin, ground

1 gallon Texas BBQ sauce

1 (6 oz) can chipotle peppers

2 tsp cayenne pepper

6 oz garlic, minced


Prepare meat and coat with salt, sugar, ground pepper, chili powder and ground cumin.

Place in refrigerator for 8 hours to marinate.

Next day, cook at 350° F until reaching an internal temperature of 165° F.

Let set on counter for 10 minutes.

Slice into 1–2 pieces, grain cut.

Add together Texas BBQ Sauce, chipotle peppers, cayenne pepper and minced garlic.

Heat sauce to 165° F and pour over meat.

Serve.


Yield: 5 servings
