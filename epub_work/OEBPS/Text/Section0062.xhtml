Cherry or Cranberry Pie

Scale this recipe Timer Shopping List Convert

2 ½ eggs

⅝ cup sugar

2 ½ oz butter, softened

⅝ cup flour

⅓ tsp baking powder

¾ cup cherries or cranberries

⅓ cup walnuts

⅝ tbsp powdered sugar

⅓ tsp vinegar


Preheat oven to 325° F. 

Mix eggs with sugar and mix well. 

Add butter to the sugar mixture and mix well. 

Add flour and baking soda; mix. 

Spray vinegar on the top of dough. 

Put walnuts and berries on an oiled pan and top with the dough mixture. 

Bake for 50 minutes at 325° F. 

Cool and top with powered sugar.


Yield: 5 servings

Gennadiy Shats
