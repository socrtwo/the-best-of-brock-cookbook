Cranberry Salsa

Scale this recipe Timer Shopping List Convert

⅜ (12 oz) bag fresh cranberries

¼ cup granulated sugar

⅛ cup fresh lime juice

1 ¼ lg garlic cloves, minced

⅓ cup fresh cilantro leaves, minced

⅝ sm jalapeño pepper, seeded, finely chopped

1 ¼ scallions, finely chopped

Salt to taste

Baked tortilla and pita chips, optional


Boil cranberries in a saucepan over high heat for 2 minutes, starting with cold water. 

Drain well. 

Combine sugar and lime juice in a bowl. 

Add cranberries and stir gently. 

Add garlic, cilantro, jalapeño and scallions (also salt to taste). 

Let "marinate" for 20 minutes. 

Serve with chips or atop cooked chicken/pork.


Yield: 5 servings ¼ cup servings

Kim Stayrook
