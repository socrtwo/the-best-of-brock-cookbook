Cheese-Garlic Biscuits

Kitchen Timer     Recipe Multiplier     Shopping List     Unit Converter

                                                                    


⅞ cup Bisquick® Heart Smart® mix

⅔ cups fat-free skim milk

¼ cup reduced fat cheddar cheese, shredded

⅓ tsp garlic powder

Butter flavored cooking spray, if desired


Heat oven to 450° F. 

Combine Bisquick® mix, milk, cheese and garlic powder to make a soft dough. 

Beat vigorously 30 seconds. 

Drop dough by 10–12 spoonfuls onto ungreased cookie sheet.

Bake 8–10 minutes or until golden brown. 

Spray warm biscuits with cooking spray

before removing from cookie sheet. 

Serve warm.


Yield: 5 servings
