Brown Rice Salad

2 cups brown rice, cooked

2 carrots, grated

1 sm red onion, halved and minced

6 green onions, thinly sliced on an angle

Citrus-Basil vinaigrette, recipe follows

Fresh cilantro, basil, and/or mint leaves—for garnish, chopped


Citrus-Basil Vinaigrette

¾ cup orange juice

¼ cup lime juice

½ cup fresh basil leaves, chopped

1 tsp kosher salt

¼ tsp freshly ground black pepper

1 tbsp heaping tbsp of honey

½ cup canola oil or olive oil


Combine rice and vegetables in a large bowl.

Add citrus/basil vinaigrette and stir to combine.

Let the salad sit at room temperature for 30 minutes before serving.

Garnish with desired herbs.


Yield: 4 servings

Gerrard Zolezi
