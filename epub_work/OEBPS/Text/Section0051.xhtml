Apple Cream Pie

2 ½ cups apple, sliced and peeled

⅓ tsp cinnamon

⅓ tsp sugar

1 ⅞ oz cream cheese, soft

1 ¼ eggs

⅜ cup sugar

⅝ (9") pie crust

1 ¼ tsps vanilla

⅓ cup brown sugar

⅛ cup flour

⅛ cup pecans, chopped


Preheat oven to 350° F.

Slice apples and sprinkle the cinnamon and ½ tsp of sugar over them.

Make cream cheese filling with cream cheese, eggs, sugar and vanilla.

Add apples to pie crust; pour cream cheese filling over apples. 

Then mix brown sugar and flour together making crumb mix with pecans and place on top.

Bake in oven at 350° F until you see it bubble, about 45 minutes.

This pie is best served when made the day before and allowed to chill overnight.


Yield: 5 servings
