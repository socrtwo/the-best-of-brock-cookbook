Rotisserie Style Chicken

Kitchen Timer     Recipe Multiplier     Shopping List     Unit Converter

                                                                    


5 chicken quarters (leg or breast)

⅞ oz McCormick's® rotisserie seasoning

⅝ oz Worcestershire sauce

⅜ oz brown sugar

⅜ oz water

⅓ oz parsley, chopped


Mix all ingredients except chicken together.

Rub and coat chicken; marinate for 24 hours. 

Cook in 350° F oven to 165° F internal temperature, about 45 minutes.


Yield: 5 servings
