Adobo Seasoned Baked Chicken Wings

Scale this recipe Timer Shopping List Convert

⅓ cup mango nectar

⅛ cup honey

⅛ cup red hot sauce

⅝ tbsp salt and to taste

⅝ tsp ground black pepper and to taste

1 ¼ tsps garlic powder

1 ¼ tsps onion powder

1 ¼ tsps ground turmeric

25 chicken wings, patted dry

1 ¼ tbsps olive oil

1 ¼ tbsps fresh oregano, chopped


Preheat oven to 425° F.

Stir together mango nectar, honey and hot sauce in a bowl; season with salt and pepper to taste. 

Set aside.


For Adobo Seasoning

Mix garlic powder, onion powder and

turmeric in a bowl. 

Add 1 tbsp of salt and 1 tsp of black pepper.

Toss wings in a bowl with olive oil and adobo seasoning mix.

Place wings onto a large baking sheet in a single layer.

Bake for 20 minutes, turn wings over and bake for 20 minutes more.

Remove wings. 

Brush with honey and hot sauce glaze and bake 20 additional minutes.

Place cooked wings on a platter and sprinkle with oregano.


Yield: 5 servings
