Tortellini With Chicken, Basil and Tomato

⅝ (2-lb) bag of tortellini

⅝ lb chicken breast, sliced

Olive oil

⅛ cup red onion, finely diced

⅓ tsp garlic, chopped

1 ¼ cups tomato, diced

⅓ cup white wine

Basil pesto, to taste

⅛ cup Parmesan cheese

1 ¼ tbsps parsley, chopped

Salt and pepper, to taste


Cook tortellini according to directions. 

In a warm sauté pan, brown off chicken in olive oil. 

When brown, add onions and garlic. 

Cook about 2 minutes. 

Add tomatoes, white wine and basil pesto. 

Once mixture starts to boil, add cooked tortellini. 

Reduce flame. 

Add Parmesan and parsley. 

Salt and pepper to taste.


Yield: 5 servings
