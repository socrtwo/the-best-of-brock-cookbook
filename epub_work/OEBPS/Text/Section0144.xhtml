Crabmeat Salad

Scale this recipe Timer Shopping List Convert

12 oz imitation crabmeat

¼ bunch celery

⅓ bunch green onions

2 med cucumbers

3 eggs

4 oz sweet fish roe

2–3 tbsp mayonnaise


Cut into small pieces the crabmeat, celery, onions, cucumber and eggs. 

Add sweet fish roe and mayonnaise; mix well. 

Add salt and pepper as needed.


Yield: 4 servings

Leonid Shteyman
