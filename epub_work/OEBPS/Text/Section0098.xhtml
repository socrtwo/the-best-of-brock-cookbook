Pizza Sauce

Scale this recipe Timer Shopping List Convert

1 cup crushed tomato

½ tbsp fresh basil, chopped

⅓ tbsp sugar

½ tbsp fresh garlic, chopped

⅓ tbsp Parmesan cheese, grated

⅜ tsp kosher salt

⅛ oz olive oil


Blend all ingredients and refrigerate. 

Use 8 oz for a 20 oz dough ball. 

Can be made with the Pizza Dough in the Breakfast and Breads section!


Yield: 5 servings

(or 16 slices of pizza considered for nutrition purposes as 1 serving each)

Tony Paterno
