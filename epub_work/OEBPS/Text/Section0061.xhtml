Cherry-O Cream Cheese Pie

Scale this recipe Timer Shopping List Convert

1 (8 oz) package cream cheese

1 (15 oz) can sweetened condensed milk

⅓ cup fresh or bottled lemon juice

⅝ tsp vanilla extract

1 (21 oz) can cherry pie filling

1 (9") graham cracker crumb crust


Soften cream cheese to room temperature; whip until fluffy. 

Gradually add condensed milk while continuing to beat until well-blended. 

Add lemon juice and vanilla extract; blend well. 

Pour into crust and chill in refrigerator for 2–3 hours before garnishing top of pie with cherry pie filling.


Yield: One 9" pie of 8 servings

Debbie O’Donovan
