Pizza Dip

Scale this recipe Timer Shopping List Convert

3 ⅓ oz cream cheese

⅞ oz tomato sauce

⅝ tbsp garlic powder

⅝ tbsp oregano

3 ⅓ oz mozzarella cheese, shredded


Preheat oven to 350° F. 

Soften cream cheese. 

Spread softened cream cheese in small 9" x 13" casserole or sauce baking dish.

In a bowl, mix tomato sauce with garlic powder. 

Spread mixture on top of cream cheese. 

Sprinkle mozzarella cheese and oregano on top of cream cheese. 

Bake at 350° F until golden brown and bubbly; approximately 12–15 minutes.


Yield: 5 servings.

William Daniel Hall
