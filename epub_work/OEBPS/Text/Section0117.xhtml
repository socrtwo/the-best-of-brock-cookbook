Easy Add-In Macaroni and Cheese

Kitchen Timer     Recipe Multiplier     Shopping List     Unit Converter

                                                                    


1 package (7 ¼ oz) macaroni and cheese

1 can (10 ¾ oz) condensed cream of mushroom soup

½ cup margarine

3 tbsp sour cream

1 cup cheddar cheese, shredded

12 buttery round crackers


Preheat oven to 350° F.

Cook the macaroni according to directions on the box. 

Remove from heat, drain.

Add soup, ¼ cup of margarine, sour cream, shredded cheese, and the cheese packet from the box. 

[Do not use milk as directed on the box.]

Pour in a small casserole dish and top with crumbled crackers. 

Melt the remaining margarine and pour over the crackers.

Bake in a preheated oven for 25 minutes.


Yield: 6 servings
