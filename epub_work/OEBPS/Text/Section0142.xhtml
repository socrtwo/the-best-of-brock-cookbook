Couscous Salad

Kitchen Timer     Recipe Multiplier     Shopping List     Unit Converter

                                                                    


1 ⅛ boxes couscous, cooked as instructed on box

¼ cup green onion, chopped

½ cup feta cheese, crumbled

½ cup tomatoes, diced

½ cup cucumbers, diced

1 cup peppers, diced

Salt, to taste

Pepper, to taste

Juice from 7 fresh lemons

⅛ cup oil


Mix all ingredients together after couscous has cooled and chilled.

Adjust seasonings if needed.


Yield: 5 servings
