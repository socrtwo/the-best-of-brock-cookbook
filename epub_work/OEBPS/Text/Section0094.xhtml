Nacho Bake

Kitchen Timer     Recipe Multiplier     Shopping List     Unit Converter

                                                                    


1 box Velveeta Shells and Cheese Dinner

1 lb ground beef

1 package taco seasoning mix

¾ cup water

¾ cup sour cream

¾ cup shredded cheese

¾ cup salsa

¼ cup Tortilla chips, crushed


Heat oven to 400° F. 

Cook Velveeta Shells and Cheese Dinner. 

Brown the ground beef. 

Add taco seasoning mix and water, cook 5 minutes. 

Mix sour cream into dinner. 

Put half of dinner into baking dish. 

Top with beef mixture, ½ cheese and remaining dinner mix. 

Top with salsa, rest of cheese and crushed tortilla chips. 

Cover. 

Bake 15 minutes. 

Bake uncovered and extra 5 minutes.


Yield: 3 servings
