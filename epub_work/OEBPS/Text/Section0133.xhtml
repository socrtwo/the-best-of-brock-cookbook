Apple Spinach Salad

Scale this recipe Timer Shopping List Convert

1 bag fresh spinach

2 granny smith apples

1 cup sunflower seeds

⅓ cup apple vinegar

¼ tsp salt

1 tsp garlic salt

1 tsp celery salt


Wash spinach and apples. 

Drain and add spinach to bowl with sliced apples and sunflower seeds.

In a separate bowl combine remaining ingredients for dressing.

Toss in spinach, apples and sunflower seeds.


Yield: 6–8 servings

Larry Stelitano
