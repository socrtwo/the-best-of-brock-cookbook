Teriyaki Burger

Kitchen Timer     Recipe Multiplier     Shopping List     Unit Converter

                                                                    


5 (3 oz) frozen burger patties

2 ¼ tbsps of Teriyaki

½ cup of bean sprouts

⅞ tbsp of Sesame oil

2 ¼ tbsps of duck sauce

⅞ onions, sliced

2 ¼ Kaiser rolls


Grill 5 (3 oz) frozen burger patties.

Sprinkle burgers with Teriyaki sauce while cooking.

Sauté 2 full onion slices with each burger.

Spread sesame oil on Kaiser rolls and toast on grill.

When each burger reaches 165° F, remove from grill and place on toasted the Kaisers.

Top with sautéed onion and raw bean sprouts.

Serve with duck sauce for dipping.


Yield: 5 servings
