Potato Salad

Scale this recipe Timer Shopping List Convert

2 ½ lbs potatoes

2 lg onions/2 oz onion powder (½ cup diced onions or 2 tbsp onion powder)

½ cup celery, diced sm

1 pint (½ cup) sweet relish

¼ cup yellow mustard

1 cup mayonnaise

Salt to taste

Pepper to taste


Cut potatoes in ¾" dice. 

Cook potatoes in boiling water until tender but firm.

Dice onion and celery.

Add all ingredients in a large bowl and mix thoroughly. 

Adjust seasoning with salt and pepper.

Allow to sit refrigerated until flavors blend well.


Yield: 5 servings ½ lbs or 66—4 oz portions

(16—4 oz portions)

Antoine Lee
