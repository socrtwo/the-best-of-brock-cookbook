Celeste’s Best BBQ Sauce

Kitchen Timer     Recipe Multiplier     Shopping List     Unit Converter

                                                                    


2 cups ketchup

2 ½ tbsp yellow mustard

2 ½ tbsp Worcestershire sauce

½ tsp liquid smoke

2 tsp smoked paprika

2 tbsp white sugar

2 tbsp brown sugar

2 tbsp honey

2 tsp apple cider vinegar

½ tsp garlic powder

Set a medium sized pot on low heat. 

Simmer all ingredients for 20–30 minutes.

Serve on any of your favorite grilled foods.


Yield: 2 ½–2 ¾ cup (or 17, 1 ¼ oz
servings for nutrition calculations.)
