Lemon Basil Smoothie

Scale this recipe Timer Shopping List Convert

2 cups lemon sorbet or sherbet

1 cup fat free milk

6 oz vanilla yogurt

1 tsp lemon zest, grated

⅓ cup fresh squeezed lemon juice

2 tbsp fresh basil, chopped

1 cup ice cubes

Lemon curl as needed


In a blender, combine until smooth, sorbet, milk, yogurt, lemon zest, lemon juice, basil and ice. 

Scrape sides once during blending. 

Pour into three glasses, garnish with additional lemon curl and basil. 

Serve immediately.


Yield: 3 servings
