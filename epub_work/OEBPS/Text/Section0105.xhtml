Cream Cheese Pie

Kitchen Timer     Recipe Multiplier     Shopping List     Unit Converter

                                                                    


Filling

1 ¼ (8 oz) packages cream cheese, softened

1 ⅞ eggs, beaten one at a time

¼ cup sugar

⅛ tsp almond extract


Icing

⅝ cup sour cream

1 ⅞ tbsps sugar

⅝ tsp vanilla extract


Preheat oven to 325° F.

Beat filling ingredients together until smooth. 

Butter an 8" or 9" pie plate. 

Pour mixture into pie plate and bake in a 325° F oven for 40 minutes.

Remove from oven and cool 20 minutes.

While cooling, mix icing ingredients into a cream consistency.

Pour icing on top of pie and bake in the oven at the same temperature for 15 minutes.

Let cool and refrigerate at least 2 hours prior to serving.


Yield: 5 servings
