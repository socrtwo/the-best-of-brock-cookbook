Home Style Baked Beans

Scale this recipe Timer Shopping List Convert

6 ⅔ oz baked beans

6 ½ oz kidney beans, rinsed and drained

6 ¼ oz butter beans, rinsed and drained

⅛ cup tomato sauce

⅛ cup catsup

⅜ onion, finely chopped

⅝ tsp dry mustard

1 ¼ cloves garlic, minced

⅓ cup brown sugar, packed

⅝ tbsp Worcestershire sauce


Preheat oven to 350° F.

Combine all ingredients together; pour into a

greased 13" x 9" baking dish. 

Bake, uncovered, at 350° F for 30 minutes, or until beans are desired consistency.


Yield: 5 servings

Donna Dunn
