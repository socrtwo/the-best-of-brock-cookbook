Heaven on a Bun

Kitchen Timer     Recipe Multiplier     Shopping List     Unit Converter

                                                                    


1 lb ground pork

1 lb ground beef

Seasoning blend to taste

1 pint sour cream

6 oz cream cheese

6–8 hamburger buns

6–8 slices jalapeño pepper cheese


Brown pork and beef in frying pan. 

Drain off grease. 

Season to taste with seasoning blend. 

Add sour cream and cream cheese. 

Stir mixture over medium-low heat until blended and warmed.

Spoon meat mixture on bottom half of hamburger bun. 

Top with slice of jalapeño pepper cheese, if desired. 

Broil just until cheese is melted. 

Add top of bun and serve. 

Some people like lettuce and tomato with it.


Yield: 6–8 servings
