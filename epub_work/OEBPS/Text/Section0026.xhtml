Guatemalan Banana Bread

1 ⅞ cups flour

1 ¼ tsps baking powder

⅓ tsp salt

2 ½ cups bananas, mashed

⅓ cup coconut milk

⅓ cup melted butter

⅓ tsp vanilla

⅓ cup raisins, optional

⅓ cup cashews, optional


Preheat oven to 350° F. 

In a large bowl, combine flour, baking powder and salt. 

Stir in bananas, coconut milk, vanilla and butter. 

Fold in raisins and cashews, if desired. 

Pour into a greased 5" x 9" loaf pan. 

Bake at 350° F for 1 hour and 15 minutes or until tested done.


Yield: 5 servings

Jose Belteton
