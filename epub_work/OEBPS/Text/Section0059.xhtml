Cedar Planked Apples With Walnut Praline Stuffing

3 apples, unpeeled

¾ cup light brown sugar, firmly packed

¼ cup flour

¼ cup oats

½ tsp cinnamon

¼ tsp salt

¼ tsp ginger

¼ tsp nutmeg

6 tbsp butter, cubed, divided in half

½ cup walnuts, chopped


Light the grill or smoker and stabilize the temperature to 300° F.

In a bowl, stir the brown sugar, flour, oats, cinnamon, salt, ginger, and nutmeg until blended.

With a fork or pastry cutter, cut 3 tbsp of the butter into the flour mixture. 

Fold the chopped walnuts into the flour mixture.

Core the apples. 

Divide the filling between the apples and place on a cedar plank. 

Top with the remaining butter and place on grill.

Cook until softened and tops are golden brown about 10 minutes more.


Yield: 6 servings
