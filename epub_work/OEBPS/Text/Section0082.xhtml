Strawberry Topping

Scale this recipe Timer Shopping List Convert

⅜ cup water

⅜ cup sugar

⅞ tbsp corn syrup

1 ¼ tbsps cornstarch

Pinch salt

Small amount of red food coloring

⅞ tbsp strawberry Jell-O®

⅜ quart fresh strawberries, halved


Cook together 1 cup of water, 1 cup of sugar, 3 tbsp of cornstarch, 2 tbsp of corn syrup, and a pinch of salt. 

Boil together until clear and thick. 

Add a small amount of red food coloring and 2 tbsp of strawberry Jell-O®. 

Cool and add berries.


Yield: 5 servings

Renee Bloch
