Bean Casserole

Scale this recipe Timer Shopping List Convert

1 lb ground beef

1 package of hot dogs

16 oz pork and beans

1 envelope of dry onion soup mix

⅓ cup ketchup

¼ cup water

2 tbsp brown sugar

1 tbsp prepared yellow mustard


Brown ground beef in skillet until no longer pink.

Slice hot dogs into bite-sized pieces.

Add all of the remaining ingredients into the skillet with the ground beef.

Stir thoroughly.

Pour all into a casserole dish.

Bake uncovered in 350° F oven for 1 hour.

Stir, then cover, and bake an additional 30–45 minutes.


Yield: 5 servings

Cheryl Teske
