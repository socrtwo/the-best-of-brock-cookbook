Chinamerica Chicken Pineapple Feast

Scale this recipe Timer Shopping List Convert

1 chicken breast

White pepper powder, to taste

½ tsp salt

½ tsp honey

½ cup pineapple, chopped

1 tbsp cooking wine

Oil

3 tbsp mayonnaise

¼ tsp salt, optional

½ tsp sugar, if needed


Pound chicken breast with a wood mallet for a short period of time to soften the breast. 

Put in bowl.

Add a little bit of white pepper powder, ½ tsp of salt and ½ tsp of honey. 

Mix everything thoroughly, turning chicken over to coat.

Chop ½ cup of pineapple and add to bowl. 

Add 1 tbsp of cooking wine. 

Mix everything together thoroughly and refrigerate about 60 minutes.

Cook chicken with some oil in a pot until brownish. 

You may use an oven instead.

Cook the pineapple sauce. 

Gradually add 3 tbsp of mayonnaise, stirring constantly.

If needed, add ¼ tsp of salt and/or ½ tsp of sugar.

Pour over chicken and serve.


Yield: 1 serving

Jie Astri
