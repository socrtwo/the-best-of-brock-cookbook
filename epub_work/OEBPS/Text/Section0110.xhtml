Mary's Zucchini Bread

Scale this recipe Timer Shopping List Convert

1 ¼ cups flour

⅞ tsp baking soda

⅝ tsp salt

⅛ tsp baking powder

1 tsp cinnamon

½ tsp nutmeg

1 ¼ eggs

⅜ cup oil

⅞ cup sugar

1 ¼ tsps vanilla

⅞ cup zucchini, shredded

⅜ cup dates, snipped

⅜ cup walnuts, broken

⅜ sm can crushed pineapple, well drained


Combine flour, baking soda, salt, baking powder, cinnamon and nutmeg in bowl; mix.

Add eggs, oil. 

Beat top until thick. 

Add sugar and vanilla and mix.

Stir in shredded zucchini, dates, broken walnuts and well-drained pineapple.

Mix all ingredients together and pour into

⅞ greased loaf pans (5" x 9") and bake at 350° F for 1 hour or until tested done in center.


Yield: 5 servings (5" x 9" pan)

Mary Parsons, mother of Debbie O'Donovan
