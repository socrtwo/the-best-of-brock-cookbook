Crab and Corn Chowder

Scale this recipe Timer Shopping List Convert

5 oz bacon, sm dice

5 oz butter

⅝ lg onion, sm dice

5 stalks celery, sm dice

⅝ tbsp garlic, minced

1 ¼ cups flour, all-purpose

1 ⅞ quarts crab stock

⅝ lb corn

7 ½ oz potatoes, sm dice

⅝ lb crab meat

⅝ tbsp OLD BAY® seasoning

Salt to taste

Ground pepper to taste

10 oz half and half


Cut all vegetables as described.

Place the bacon and the butter into a soup pot. 

Cook to render the fat from the bacon. 

Cook until the bacon is browned, then remove.

Add the onion, celery and garlic. 

Cook until the onions are translucent.

Add the flour and mix well. 

Cook the vegetable flour mixture on low for about 5 minutes. 

Keep a stir on the mixture.

Add the stock to the vegetable/flour mixture while whisking. 

Bring the soup to a simmer.

Add the corn and the potatoes. 

Continue to simmer until the potatoes are tender.

Add the crab meat, OLD BAY®, salt and pepper. 

Slowly add the half and half while whisking.


Yield: 5 servings

Jerry Goard
